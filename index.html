How To Keep Busy During Quarantine:

Take A Free Online Course From An Ivy League University

 



There are over 450 classes being offered online from various Ivy League Institutions. Classes are free to audit via EdX and Coursera. Topics range from healthcare to business to coding! They've got it all- take the time to brush up on your skills or to learn something new.

They've also got some great tips on how to stay focused and organized with your classes and work during Quarantine- check those out, too!

https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/ivy-league-free-online-courses-a0d7ae675869/
 

Free Virtual Museum Tours
 


Various famous museums across the globe are offering free virtual tours.

Here's a few museums and the links to their tours:

Musée d’Orsay, Paris 

Walk through one of the Musée d'Orsay's most popular galleries housing dozens of the works of famous French artists. This gallery features works from 1848 to 1914 from artists like Monet, Cézanne, and Gauguin to name just a few!

The Louvre

Save yourself a trip to Paris and take advantage of free virtual tours offered by the Louvre, one of the world’s most famous art museums! Some exhibits offered online include Egyptian Antiquities, Remains of the Louvre’s Moat, The Advent of the Artist, and Galerie d’Apollon.

National Gallery of Art, Washington, D.C.

The National Gallery of Art offers two online exhibits through Google. In the first exhibit, check out the history of American Fashion from 1740 to 1895. Then be sure to head over to the second exhibit to get a peek at a collection of Dutch Baroque artists like famous painter, Johannes Vermeer.
